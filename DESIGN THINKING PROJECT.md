## My Reflection
Working on this project allowed me to step into the shoes of autistic and ADHD individuals and their caregivers, understanding the daily challenges they face. Designing a non-functional prototype challenged me to prioritize usability, accessibility, and empathy over technical coding. I learned the importance of clear navigation, visual clarity, and supportive features that make technology inclusive. This experience deepened my appreciation for human-centered design and reinforced that impactful solutions start with understanding real-world needs.
